Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 10, 2026

Medicine & Health Reminder

Medicine & Health Reminder (“the App”) helps users manage medicine schedules, health reminders, and personal medical records.

Information We Collect

The App may store the following information:

  • Medicine schedules and reminders
  • Health-related notes and records
  • Medical images or attachments added by the user
  • App preferences and settings

By default, this information is stored locally on the user's own device only.

The App does not upload or store user data on developer-owned servers.

If the user chooses to sign in with Google and use the optional backup feature, backup data is stored only inside the user's own private Google Drive AppData folder.

Google Drive backup and restore are completely optional and require explicit user consent and sign-in.

Google Sign-In and Google Drive Access

The App optionally allows users to sign in with Google in order to backup and restore their medicine reminders and health records.

The App uses the Google Drive drive.appdata scope only.

  • Data is stored privately in the user's own Google Drive account
  • Data is not shared with third parties
  • Data is not sold
  • Only the user can access their backup data
  • The App cannot access general Google Drive files

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share user data with third parties.

User Control

  • Users may use the app without Google Sign-In
  • Users may revoke Google account access anytime through Google Account settings
  • Users may delete backups from Google Drive
  • Users may uninstall the app to remove local device data

Permissions Used

  • Internet access — backup and restore
  • Notifications and alarms — medicine reminders
  • Camera/media access — medical image attachments
  • Boot completed — restore reminders after device restart
